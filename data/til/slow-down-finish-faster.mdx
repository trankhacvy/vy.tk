## Definition of Done:

- Done is boolean, it's either done or it's not. There is not "90% done"
- Done mean no go back

## To make something done faster, you need to slow down

- Understand all the variables at play
  - Need to clarify all requirements
  - Don't assume
  - Ask everything you need
- Understand who are the character involved
- Understand the requirement for requirement
